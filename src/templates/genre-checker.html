Genre Checker

The Genre Checker will allow you to identify the genre of books by simply inputting the title and summary of a text, and it will then provide you with the most suitable genre based on the information.
Note:
   1. The accuracy of the genre checker will vary depending on the quality of provided text. Thus, providing more substantial summary will make the prediction more accurate.
   2. The checker may occassionaly produce incorrect results.
